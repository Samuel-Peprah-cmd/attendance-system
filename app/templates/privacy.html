Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how ATOM Gate collects, uses, stores, protects, and discloses personal data in connection with its school security, attendance, identity, communication, and related services.

📅

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Introduction

ATOM Gate is committed to handling personal data responsibly and in accordance with applicable laws, internal controls, and school service requirements. This policy applies to users of the ATOM Gate website, platform, scanner applications, communication services, and related administrative systems.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect and process information including, but not limited to:

  • Student identity records, including name, class, code, and ID details
  • Staff records, including name, role, contact information, and attendance logs
  • Guardian or parent details, including names, emails, and phone numbers
  • Attendance records, timestamps, entry/exit events, and related operational logs
  • Device and scanner information used for security operations
  • Location-related data where enabled for scan verification and reporting
  • Uploaded files, attachments, school notices, and communication records
  • Billing, subscription, and transaction-related data

3. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide attendance, identity, and security services
  • Generate and verify student and staff ID credentials
  • Send guardian, school, or staff notifications
  • Support school administration and operational reporting
  • Manage billing, subscriptions, and service delivery
  • Improve system performance, reliability, and security
  • Detect misuse, unauthorized access, or policy violations

4. Legal and Operational Basis

We process data based on lawful operational needs, contractual service obligations, compliance requirements, and legitimate interests in providing secure and effective platform services. Schools remain responsible for ensuring that their use of ATOM Gate aligns with their own legal and institutional obligations.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share data only where necessary for service operation, compliance, payment processing, communications delivery, cloud storage, security enforcement, or as required by law.

6. Data Retention

We retain data for as long as reasonably necessary to provide services, support school operations, maintain legal or compliance records, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and preserve audit trails. Retention periods may vary depending on the nature of the information and service relationship.

7. Security

We implement administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no digital system is entirely risk-free, and users should also take reasonable steps to protect their credentials and access channels.

8. User Rights and Requests

Where applicable, users or institutions may request access, correction, clarification, or review of certain data handled through the platform, subject to operational, legal, and contractual limitations.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

10. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, schools and users may contact ATOM Gate through the official support or business communication channels provided on the platform.
